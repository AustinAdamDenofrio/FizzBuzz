Fizz Buzz: Code Challenge

It's Fizz Buzz! The well know coding challenge. We take the once common coding challenge and make ours a more dynamic challenge by adding the ability to control the lenth of numbers we loop through and dynamically change the "FIZZ" and "BUZZ" values based on the users input. Built in are several fail safes, so feel free to try and input invalid numbers.

Instructions

Choose 2 values, a lower and a higher number, and click submit. The app will run it's brainy calculations and display a list of numbers and and keywords stating from 1 and going until it reaches the End Number. Here are the keywords that will be displayed and their meaning:

  • Fizz:: The number reached in the sequence is evenly divisible by Fizz
  • Buzz:: The number reached in the sequence is evenly divisible by Buzz
  • FizzBuzz:: The number reached in the sequence is evenly divisible by both Fizz and Buzz
  • Any Number:: Any number that is neither divisible by Fizz or Buzz without remaineder.
